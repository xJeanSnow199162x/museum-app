Overview

An in-depth installation of Jean-Michel Basquiat, opening May 26, 2021, features all 13 works by the artist in the Broad collection, including three works on view for the first time at The Broad: Santo 2 (1982),Deaf (1984),and Wicker (1984). An outcome of sustained and ongoing engagement with artists and artwork, the Broad collection is distinctive in its exceptional dedication to the full arc of artists’careers.The Broad has highlighted this depth through expansive presentations in the third floor galleries,featuring Jean-Michel Basquiat,Roy Lichtenstein,Kara Walker,Andy Warhol,and Christopher Wool, along with works by many others.
